How to claim an URL

How to claim?

First embed your CoinAwesome address anywhere inside your website. The wallet scans through the HTML page and finds all addresses. Currently by default the first one is taken as the donation address.

After you have embedded your address press the 'claim now' button inside here while on your site.

For advanced users we offer also an API for claiming.

You can claim the page only once every 3 hours.
